## Deploy to Vercel (5 minutes)

1. Go to https://vercel.com and sign up for free (use GitHub, Google, or email)
2. Click **"Add New Project"**
3. Click **"Upload"** (you don't need GitHub)
4. Drag and drop the entire `newsboard` folder
5. Click **Deploy** â€” done! You'll get a URL like `newsboard.vercel.app`

## Add to iPhone Home Screen

1. Open your Vercel URL in **Safari** (must be Safari, not Chrome)
2. Tap the **Share button** (box with arrow pointing up)
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Name it "NewsBoard" and tap **Add**
5. The app icon appears on your home screen ðŸŽ‰

## Features
- Live RSS feeds from BBC, TechCrunch, Bloomberg
- Side-by-side columns, swipe between them
- Reorder or remove columns
- Add more sources (Reuters, The Verge, CNBC, Ars Technica, The Guardian, WSJ)
- Bookmark articles
- Full-text search
- Works offline (cached)
- Auto-refreshes every 15 minutes
- Your preferences saved locally on your phone
